LAHAINAH DIGERMO

Hi, my name is Lahainah P. Digermo, or you can call me Lai or Lahai. I am currently a student in the University of Baguio. I graduated elementary school in University of Baguio Laboratory Elementary school. I graduated highschool and senior highschool in University of Baguio Science High School I'm currently 19 years old, and I was born on June 23, 2004. I have one older brother who is currently working in Alphaland Baguio and a graduate of University of Baguio with a Bachelor Degree in Civil Engineering, and I have one younger sister who is currently I am studying at the University of Baguio Science High School. I'm born and raised here in Baguio. My mother is from Bangui, Ilocos Norte, and my father is from Marawi. In holidays we always visit my tito in Rosario, La Union but sometimes we travel to Ilocos Norte, Bangui to meet my relatives.

It was really fun, and the coaches said that I had potential, but I had to go back to swimming because archery sports are very expensive. I also do aquathlon. which is the combination of swimming and running. Before the pandemic, I was training to swim, run, and bike to join the SEA Games in Cambodia. I tried out for the Philippine team in triathlon, but sadly, I didn't continue it because I lost my passion. I'm currently training swimming so I could join this years BBEAL.
